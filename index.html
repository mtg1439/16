Instructions:

Welcome to 16! Your objective is to turn all squares on the board black.

Click the 'Begin' button to start the game.

When you click a square, it will toggle its color (white to black, or black to white). Additionally, the square on the mirror opposite end of the grid will also toggle its color.

Beware! Each level introduces a "mystery square". This square, when clicked, will not only toggle itself and its mirror opposite, but also a third unique square on the grid. The mystery squares accumulate with each level.

Complete all 10 levels to win!
